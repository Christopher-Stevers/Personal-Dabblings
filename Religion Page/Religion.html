Long Live Christ the King!

I am a Catholic who tries to take is his faith seriously.

I was introduced to traditional Catholicism from an early age, and embraced it from that time forward, despite some shameful falls. I belong to the Roman Catholic Church founded by Christ. I do not accept claimant to the papacy due to his own heresy, and the heresey of his past few predecessors. Therefore, I still adhere to Catholicism as it has been taught for the past two millenia, not the watered down imposter which is seen in "Catholic" Churches today.

With the help of Fr. Saunders and Father Bernard OSB I became quite interested in spiritual reading. Father Edward Leen provide's excellent works, however my favourite spirtual work is still the Imitation of Christ

Currently I attend Our Lady of Victory Church in London Ontario, where my half-brother, Father Jeremy Saunders is pastor.

I also belong to an online chat community of young sedevacantists on the platform, "Discord." If you are a young sedevacantist and are interested in this community feel free to contact me
