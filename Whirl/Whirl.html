About us

We are a certified organic farm, and raise pork, grass-fed beef and chicken. All animals have access to the outdoors. Our cattle are a mixture of (mainly) Angus, Limousin, and Blonde Aquataine. They are entirely Grass-fed, and spend the warmer half of the year on pasture. During the winter months they fed grass and alfalfa hay as well as baleage, which is usually oats or rye. While they are not allowed to access pasture during the winter, they are do have a corral which they very much enjoy.
